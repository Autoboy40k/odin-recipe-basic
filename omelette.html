Cheese and Onion Omelette

Recipe feeds one person. Enjoy with hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 large onion
  • 1-4 cloves of garlic depending on taste
  • 100-150g cheddar cheese
    • Photo by Anna Tis

Method

  1. Crack 3 eggs into a bowl or jug and whisk until frothy.
  2. Coarsely chop onion, grate cheese, crush garlic.
  3. heat small frying pan with small knob of butter.
  4. pour in whisked eggs, then as edges begin to cook push them back into centre to create ridges
  5. when only centre is gooey, put all ingredients on half of the circle and fold
  6. turn heat to low and flip omelette when it is nearly cooked.
