Cottage Pie

Recipe feeds four people. Enjoy with your favourite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 6 large potatoes
  • 500g beef mince (5% fat)
  • 1 large onion
  • 1-4 cloves of garlic depending on taste
  • worcestershire sauce
  • 100-500g cheddar cheese
  1. Peel and slice potatoes. boil potatoes in salted water
  2. allow potatoes to sit in water with heat off and chop onion finely
  3. crush garlic with knife, garlic press or mortar and pestle
  4. fry mince and onion simultaneously, breaking up mince and turning to cook evenly
  5. add garlic and worcestershire sauce to mince, turn down heat and cover.
  6. drain potatoes and mash well, adding 50 - 250g cheese whilst mashing.
  7. add mince to suitable oven dish, covering with mash. add remaining cheese and cook on medium heat till brown on top.
  8. Allow to cool for 15 minutes before serving.
