Spaghetti Bolognese

Recipe feeds four people. Enjoy with your favourite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 200g of wholemeal spaghetti
  • 500g beef mince (5% fat)
  • 1 large onion
  • 1-4 cloves of garlic depending on taste
  • worcestershire sauce
  • 100-500g cheddar cheese
    • Photo by Pixabay

Method

  1. Fry mince and onion simultaneously, breaking up mince and turning over to cook evenly.
  2. Add garlic and worcestershire sauce to mince, turn down heat then cover.
  3. Boil salted water and add spaghetti. Cook until soft.
  4. Serve with grated cheese on top.
